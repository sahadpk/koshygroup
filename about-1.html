About Company

Company Profile
Koshy Group of Companies, founded in 1995 as Liwa International General Contracting LLC in Dubai, has evolved into a leading regional entity with a strong presence across the UAE and globally. Operating through specialized subsidiaries such as Wahat Lewa Contracting LLC in Dubai, and various branches in Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al-Quwain, Cameroon, India, and Central Africa, Koshy Group excels in engineering, procurement, construction, and comprehensive infrastructure services, including electrical, mechanical, HVAC, and landscaping works. With ISO certifications (ISO 9001:2009, OHSAS 18001:2007, ISO 9001-2000) affirming our commitment to quality, health, safety, and environmental standards, we deliver projects on time and on budget, surpassing expectations with the support of 1,500 skilled staff, advanced machinery, and specialized facilities. Koshy Group continues to expand its vision and leadership in the construction industry throughout the region.
Quality and Safety
At Liwa, quality and safety are integral to our operations and define our road to success. We are committed to delivering top-tier construction services at competitive prices, ensuring timely, on-budget project completion through a meticulous quality control program spanning procurement, execution, and inspection. Our adherence to engineering specifications and project-specific quality plans ensures excellence at every stage. Equally, we prioritize safety as a shared responsibility, fostering a culture of preventive practices and strict compliance with safety standards across all levels—employees, contractors, and visitors alike. By integrating comprehensive training, supervision, and accountability, we aim to achieve zero injuries and instill a company-wide commitment to safe behavior.
Core Values
Since its inception, Koshy Group has been guided by core values that shape our culture and decision-making, enabling us to deliver advanced solutions and services that create lasting value for our customers. By actively listening, understanding, and anticipating client needs, we build enduring relationships founded on trust and collaboration. Embracing the belief that challenges can only be solved through deep understanding, we foster a team spirit that transcends cultural, functional, and organizational boundaries, driving innovation and embracing ideas that challenge convention. We attract top talent, promote a supportive and safe environment, and uphold honesty, ethics, and fairness in all we do—delivering on promises, learning from mistakes, and staying true to our values: safety first, customer focus, seamless teamwork, respect for all, integrity, innovation, and value orientation.
