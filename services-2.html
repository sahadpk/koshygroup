Industrial Infrastructure

Koshy Group specializes in constructing large-scale industrial complexes, including processing plants and factories, with a focus on structural integrity and operational suitability. With expertise to meet diverse project requirements, we have successfully delivered projects for major clients. Our current project includes warehouse construction in Sharjah.

Residential Infrastructure

Our residential construction includes building and selling individual and multi-family dwellings. The market fragments include into single-unit, manufactural duplex, quad-plex and apartments. Our dedication towards solution and effective use of resources is focused on delivering construction projects on time, on budget and up to client’s convenience.

Educational & Social Infrastructure

With our solid resources, skills, expertise, ability and commitment we understand the client’s specific requirements and effectively work for the educational and social infrastructures. Koshy group help the clients optimize the quality of social infrastructures, with minimal costs and keeping projects on scheme.

Commercial & Retail Infrastructure

Our projects include design and build of new space, refurbishment, interior fit-out and extension projects for some of the UAE, India, central Africa, Canada, UK and Cameroon clients. We have significant experience working within the retail environment and offer a completely success approach often required by our clients.
